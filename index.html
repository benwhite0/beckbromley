Welcome

This website offers empowerment strategies that you can choose to watch, listen and learn from.

Our aim is to offer guidance, based on lived experience.

These two short films, printable book and safety plan were created with purpose and passion following Beck Bromley's death by suicide aged 19.

Know that you are not alone if you ever doubt your will to live.

Please watch the films and create your own safety plan.

Print a hard copy of the safety plan guide and put it in your first aid kit at home, school or workplace.

Share with your family, friends and neighbours.

One day, this might save YOUR life,
or the life of someone YOU love.
